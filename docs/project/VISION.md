# VISION — Train or Be Trained

## Purpose
Train or Be Trained helps people quickly set up AI to work the way they actually think. It solves the gap between generic assistant behavior and real user working style by running a short self-assessment and generating a reusable Working With Me document.

## North Star
In 1-3 years, Train or Be Trained becomes the default first step before serious AI use: a lightweight setup that reliably improves assistant behavior for real tasks, not toy demos. Success means users can carry one Working With Me artifact across assistants and get noticeably better collaboration.

## User Promise
Users get a short, practical experience that outputs clear instructions they can paste into AI settings immediately. The Working With Me artifact should produce behavior that feels personal, coherent, and useful in day-to-day work, without requiring users to become prompt engineers.

## Non-Goals
- We are NOT building a political philosophy game or civic education product.
- We are NOT claiming psychometric diagnosis or deep personality science.
- We are NOT launching a broad personality platform in this phase.
- We are NOT building a course business before product validation.
- We accept the constraint that coherence in mixed profiles must be proven before beta.
