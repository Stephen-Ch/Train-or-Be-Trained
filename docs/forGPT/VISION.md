# VISION — Rawls Game

## Purpose
Rawls Game helps people explore their political values through engagement with real-world dilemmas inspired by John Rawls' theory of justice. The project solves the problem of abstract political philosophy feeling disconnected from lived experience by making values exploration interactive, reflective, and personalized.

## North Star
In 1-3 years, Rawls Game becomes a trusted tool for civic education, helping users understand their own values and engage more thoughtfully with political debates. We aim to support educators, community organizers, and individuals seeking clarity on what matters most to them when considering social justice questions.

## User Promise
Users will experience a judgment-free space to explore political values through structured dilemmas, receive personalized reflection on their positions, and understand where they align with different political philosophies—without being told what to think.

## Non-Goals
- We are NOT building a political advocacy platform or endorsing specific policies
- We are NOT creating a social network or debate forum
- We are NOT tracking users across sessions without explicit consent
- We are NOT monetizing user data or political profiles
- We accept the constraint that values exploration must remain private and non-judgmental
