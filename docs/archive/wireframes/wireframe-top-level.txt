Behavior & Rules
1) Render ALL top-level questions for the active category in one scrollable view.
2) Gating: Continue is disabled until every top-level question has a selected value.
3) On Continue: navigate to the Deeper-Dive flow for the FIRST top-level (e.g., A1).
4) Persistence: Answers save immediately to SessionStore; refresh resumes with selected values.
5) Re-entry (from Review/Edit): land on this view for the chosen category.
6) A11y: All radio groups use <fieldset><legend> or aria-labelledby linked to statement.
7) Performance: If >10 TLQs, use lightweight virtual scroll or lazy rendering.

Selectors (stable)
- Header:         data-testid="category-title", "category-progress"
- TLQ card:       data-testid="tlq-card-<id>"
- TLQ statement:  data-testid="tlq-statement-<id>"
- Likert radios:  data-testid="likert-<id>-value-1..5"
- Continue:       data-testid="continue"
