# Working With Me — Vision

## What This Is

A short self-assessment that generates a personal document you can give any AI assistant — so it knows how to work with *you*, not the average person.

In five to twenty minutes, it asks honest questions about how you actually think and work. At the end, it produces a plain markdown document — your personal AI trainer — that you paste once into Claude's custom instructions, ChatGPT's profile settings, or any AI you use regularly.

The document travels. It works across every AI platform. Nothing is stored. The whole thing runs in your browser.

## What It Is Not

- Not a personality assessment. No types, no labels.
- Not a diagnostic tool. No clinical claims.
- Not a game. The output is a working document, not a revelation.
- Not platform-locked.

## The Output

A plain markdown document, typically 300–600 words, structured as direct instructions to an AI assistant. Written in the second person — addressed to the AI — so it can be pasted without editing.

## Architecture

Angular PWA, JSON content model, client-side only. No backend, no account, no data storage. Based on the Rawls architecture pattern.
